What we do

Ceilings

Countrywide Ceilings specializes in high-quality customized ceilings for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. We offer a wide variety of ceiling solutions that suit different styles and project requirements.

Our ceiling options include suspended ceilings, PVC ceilings, flush plaster ceilings, and many other modern ceiling systems.

Bulkhead Designs

Countrywide Ceilings (CWC) believes that the sky is the limit; therefore, we never limit ourselves. We also specialize in bulkhead design, which is highly preferred by customers who want to beautify their homes.

A bulkhead is a framed box-like structure used to fill the gap between flat panel cabinets and the ceiling. Its primary purpose is to conceal plumbing or structural elements while also giving your home a modern and stylish look. We design and install all types of bulkheads according to customer requirements.

Drywall

Drywall is commonly used in the interior of buildings, homes, and commercial spaces. These drywall systems are used as partitions to divide spaces or rooms into different areas.

Drywall, also known as gypsum board, has many advantages over traditional plaster, with the main benefit being the ease of installation.

When used to create walls, drywall panels are attached to wall studs with screws. They are then taped and finished with joint compound where the panels meet.

Various textures can be applied to drywall to create an attractive finish, including smooth or textured surfaces.

Venetian Plaster & Stucco

When it comes to luxurious and timeless wall finishes, Venetian plaster stands in a class of its own. Known for its rich texture, versatility, and historical significance, Venetian plaster has become a popular choice for homeowners, designers, and architects.

Venetian plaster offers excellent durability and breathability. Unlike paint, it allows walls to breathe naturally. It is commonly applied in kitchens, bathrooms, and interior walls.

Achieving a professional finish requires an experienced and skilled artisan to ensure the plaster is applied correctly and produces the desired results.

Venetian plaster has gained significant attention in modern interior design because of its durability and beauty. Its texture creates a unique, almost three-dimensional appearance that can transform a simple wall into a work of art.
