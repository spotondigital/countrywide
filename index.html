Ceilings

Countrywide Ceilings specializes in high-quality customized ceilings for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. We offer a wide variety of ceiling solutions that suit different styles and project requirements.

Bulkhead Designs

A bulkhead is a framed box-like structure used to fill the gap between flat panel cabinets and the ceiling. Its primary purpose is to conceal plumbing or structural elements while also giving your home a modern and stylish look.

Drywall

Drywall is commonly used in the interior of buildings, homes, and commercial spaces. These drywall systems are used as partitions to divide spaces or rooms into different areas.

Venetian Plaster & Stucco

Venetian plaster offers excellent durability and breathability. Unlike paint, it allows walls to breathe naturally. It is commonly applied in kitchens, bathrooms, and interior walls.
